Octal To Decimal conversion Questions

These questions are generally asked in university exam

21. Convert the octal number 1674 to decimal.

Solution: 940

  • Write the place values: 1 × 8³, 6 × 8², 7 × 8¹, 4 × 8⁰.
  • Calculate the powers: 1 × 512, 6 × 64, 7 × 8, 4 × 1.
  • Add the results: 512 + 384 + 56 + 4 = 940.

22. Convert the decimal number 976 to octal.

Solution: 1700

  • Divide 976 by 8: 976 ÷ 8 = 122 remainder 0.
  • Divide 122 by 8: 122 ÷ 8 = 15 remainder 2.
  • Divide 15 by 8: 15 ÷ 8 = 1 remainder 7.
  • Divide 1 by 8: 1 ÷ 8 = 0 remainder 1.
  • Read remainders from bottom to top: 1700.
