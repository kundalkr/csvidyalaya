Octal To Decimal conversion Questions

These questions are generally asked in university exam

11. Convert the octal number 645 to decimal.

Solution: 421

  • Write the place values: 6 × 8², 4 × 8¹, 5 × 8⁰.
  • Calculate the powers: 6 × 64, 4 × 8, 5 × 1.
  • Add the results: 384 + 32 + 5 = 421.

12. Convert the decimal number 550 to octal.

Solution: 1046

  • Divide 550 by 8: 550 ÷ 8 = 68 remainder 6.
  • Divide 68 by 8: 68 ÷ 8 = 8 remainder 4.
  • Divide 8 by 8: 8 ÷ 8 = 1 remainder 0.
  • Divide 1 by 8: 1 ÷ 8 = 0 remainder 1.
  • Read remainders from bottom to top: 1046.
