Question 23. Convert octal 677 to hexadecimal.

Solution: 1BF

  • Convert octal 677 to binary: 677 (octal) = 110 111 111 (binary).
  • Group binary digits in sets of 4: 0001 1011 1111.
  • Convert to hexadecimal: 0001 (1), 1011 (B), 1111 (F) = 1BF (hex).
