Question 12. Convert octal 643 to hexadecimal.

Solution: 143

  • Convert octal 643 to binary: 643 (octal) = 110 100 011 (binary).
  • Group binary digits in sets of 4: 0110 1000 0011.
  • Convert to hexadecimal: 0110 (6), 1000 (8), 0011 (3) = 143 (hex).
