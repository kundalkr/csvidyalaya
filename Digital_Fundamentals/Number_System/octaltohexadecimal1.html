Question 1. Convert octal 127 to hexadecimal.

Solution: 57

  • Convert octal 127 to binary: 127 (octal) = 001 010 111 (binary).
  • Group binary digits in sets of 4: 0001 0101 0111.
  • Convert to hexadecimal: 0001 (1), 0101 (5), 0111 (7) = 57 (hex).
